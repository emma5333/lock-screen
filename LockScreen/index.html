Daily Lock

Daily Lock is a lockscreen of the day service

Each day's lockscreen is available at LockScreen

For automatic updating:

iOS

  1. Download this shortcut on your phone.

    Due to how Apple sets wallpaper you will need to determine a scale factor for your device. Apple, by default, will zoom in any wallpaper used. So the shortcut zooms out the wallpaper by the scale factor.

  2. After importing the shortcut, open it (by clicking on the three dots in the upper right corner of the shortcut).
    The first number at the top of the shortcut is the scale factor.
    Change that number (by clicking on it) to the appropriate value.

    The scale factor is generation and iOS specific, that is, an iPhone 7 has a different value than an iPhone 10.
    Here is a list of known scale factors:
    All iOS 14 1.0
    iPhone 7 1.19
    iPhone 10 1.14
    If your phone is not in the list, you can find your scale factor by trying different numbers until your lockscreen looks correct.
    The correct number will likely be in between 1.00 and 1.20.

  3. Click on the shortcut to set your lockscreen. To make it fully automatic:
    First, run the shortcut
    Click the Automation tab on the bottom of the shortcuts app.
    Click Create Personal Automation
    Choose your automation type (I use Time of Day -> Sunrise)
    Click Next
    Search for and click on Run Shortcut on the bottom of the screen
    Choose the Daily Lock shortcut
    Click Next
    Turn off Ask Before Running
    Click Done
    Nice!
